Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At Jerusalem Signet, we respect the sacred nature of our work and the trust you place in us. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can request access or deletion.

“The protection of your personal details is as vital to us as the preservation of the sacred texts we inscribe.”

1. Information We Collect

  • Order fulfillment: name, shipping address, and contact details (to deliver your handcrafted artifact).
  • Customization details: names, dates, and dedication text you request for hand-inscription.
  • Technical data: basic diagnostics such as browser type and approximate location (e.g., via IP) to improve site performance and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To communicate about commissions and deliver your order.
  • To provide customer support through your messages and inquiries.
  • To improve site reliability and protect against misuse.

3. Third-Party Services

We may work with secure third-party providers for payment processing and shipping. Jerusalem Signet does not store your payment card details on this site. Shipping information is shared only as needed to fulfill delivery.

4. Internal Automation (Pinterest)

We operate an internal tool, Jerusalem_Signet_upload_pin, that uses the Pinterest API to publish educational and heritage-focused content. This automation is used for content distribution and does not access or store your customer payment details.

5. Data Preservation & Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We retain information only for as long as needed to fulfill orders, comply with applicable obligations, and support customer requests.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to the personal data we hold about you, or request deletion of order-related records (subject to any legal or operational requirements). Contact us via the Connect page.

7. Consent

By using our website, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
